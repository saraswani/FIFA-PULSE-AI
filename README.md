⚽ FIFA Pulse AI – Predict Tomorrow's Crowd Today

An AI-powered Digital Twin platform for FIFA World Cup 2026 that predicts crowd movement before it happens, enabling organizers to make smarter, faster, and safer decisions.

🌍 Overview

Managing tens of thousands of fans inside a stadium is one of the biggest challenges during global sporting events.

Traditional monitoring systems only react after congestion occurs.

FIFA Pulse AI changes that.

Using Generative AI, predictive analytics, and a Digital Twin of the stadium, the platform forecasts crowd behavior before congestion happens and recommends real-time operational actions for stadium officials.

Instead of asking:

"What is happening?"

the platform answers:

"What will happen next?"
